AI doesn't work the same way in every industry.
That's the point.

Every industry has different operations, different data, different bottlenecks. We learn the specifics of yours before building anything. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Fintech & Banking

Compliance is slow.
AI makes it instant.

Financial services are drowning in manual review — KYC, transaction monitoring, document verification. These are pattern-recognition tasks at scale. That's exactly what AI is built for.

KYC & Onboarding

Document extraction, identity verification, risk scoring — automated end to end. What takes a compliance team hours happens in seconds. The agent reads the document, cross-references against watchlists, flags anomalies, and produces a decision-ready report.

Transaction Monitoring

Real-time pattern analysis across transaction streams. Instead of batch reports at end of day, AI flags suspicious activity as it happens — velocity anomalies, geographic inconsistencies, behavioral deviations. Fewer false positives, faster response.

Customer Operations

Account inquiries, dispute resolution, product questions — an AI agent that knows your products, your policies, and your customer's history. Handles the repetitive volume. Escalates what actually needs a human.

Healthcare

Clinicians shouldn't spend their time on paperwork.

Healthcare operations are heavy on documentation, triage, and coordination. Most of that work follows predictable patterns — the kind AI handles well. We put clinicians back on clinical work.

Patient Triage

AI that reads symptoms, medical history, and urgency signals to route patients correctly. Not a replacement for clinical judgment — a filter that ensures the right cases get to the right people faster. Works across chat, phone intake, and web forms.

Clinical Documentation

Encounter notes, discharge summaries, referral letters — generated from structured data and physician input. Reduces documentation time from hours to minutes. Outputs conform to your templates and compliance requirements.

Administrative Automation

Appointment scheduling, insurance verification, prior authorization workflows. These processes have clear rules and high volume — ideal for AI. The system handles the standard flow and escalates exceptions to staff.

Retail & E-commerce

Every customer interaction is an operation.

Retail at scale means thousands of simultaneous conversations — returns, tracking, product questions, complaints. Each one follows a pattern. AI handles the pattern. Your team handles the exceptions.

Customer Support

An agent that knows your product catalog, your return policies, your shipping status in real time. Handles order tracking, returns, exchanges, and product questions across every channel — web chat, WhatsApp, email. Resolves the standard cases. Routes the complex ones.

Product Intelligence

AI that understands your catalog deeply — not just keywords, but attributes, compatibility, use cases. Powers recommendation engines, search, and conversational product discovery. Customers describe what they need, the system finds it.

Operations & Fulfillment

Inventory alerts, reorder triggers, supplier communication, exception handling — automated workflows that keep the operation running without manual intervention. When something breaks the pattern, the system flags it and routes it to the right person.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Move faster with the same infrastructure.

Logistics is coordination at scale — carriers, warehouses, drivers, customers, exceptions. Every delay costs money. AI doesn't eliminate complexity, but it processes it in real time instead of after the fact.

Demand Forecasting

AI that reads historical patterns, seasonal signals, market data, and real-time sales to predict demand before it materializes. Less overstock, fewer stockouts. Forecasts update continuously, not monthly.

Carrier Coordination

Automated communication with carriers — booking, tracking updates, exception handling, SLA monitoring. When a shipment is delayed, the system detects it, notifies the right people, and triggers contingency workflows. No one checks a spreadsheet.

Warehouse Operations

Pick-path optimization, inventory reconciliation, receiving automation. AI that processes warehouse data in real time to reduce handling time, minimize errors, and keep inventory counts accurate without manual cycle counts.

Different industry.
Same discipline.

We learn your operation, find where AI fits, build the system, and run it. The vertical changes. The approach doesn't.
